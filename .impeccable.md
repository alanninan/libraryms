## Design Context

### Users
Both library members (patrons) and library staff (librarians) use the system daily. Members browse books, make reservations, check fines. Librarians manage the full catalogue, borrowings, and member accounts.

### Brand Personality
Refined. Literary. Institutional.

### Aesthetic Direction
Editorial library — literary magazine meets fine bookshop. Deep forest green sidebar as the brand anchor, warm ivory page background, amber accents for active states. Cormorant Garamond for headings at dramatic scale contrasts with DM Sans for UI text. Warm, considered, not corporate. Anti-reference: generic SaaS dashboard (cyan/purple gradients, glassmorphism, neon).

### Design Principles
1. **Typography leads** — Cormorant Garamond serif headings at light weight create the editorial character; DM Sans handles UI clarity
2. **Warm over cold** — ivory background and warm-tinted neutrals instead of pure white and zinc grays
3. **The sidebar is the brand** — deep forest green (oklch 0.24 0.065 155) with amber active states anchors every page
4. **Space is editorial** — generous padding, uppercase tracking-widest labels for data tables, serif numerals at 4xl for stats
5. **Status badges with borders** — subtle bg + matching border instead of heavy pill colors
